Grading Methods

Our platform supports different grading methods to accommodate various needs:

  • Curve Grading: Adjusts the grades based on the distribution of scores among the students.
  • Grade Boundaries: Sets predefined percentage ranges for each grade (e.g., A, B, C, etc.).
